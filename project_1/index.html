Home

For my first project, I wanted to introduce myself and give you some information about me.

So, hello! I'm Logan and I'm a junior. I have been going to EPS since I was in 5th grade and I feel super old because of it. I like to make fun of pretty much all of my friends that came after 6th grade for not being part of the "OG group," but at this point, we've all been here for a pretty long time. My experience with web design is limited to the mandatory WEB class 8th graders take as well as Web Design 1, which I took two years ago. You can imagine this lengthy break has caused me to forget most of my knowledge. However, do not worry! That is the whole point of this website, and by the time I finish it, I should be all caught up to speed.

About this website, it is very simple with an overall "get to know me" theme. The home page you are on currently will just house my introduction and some fun facts about me. I also wanted to share my interests and activities that I do outside of school, which you can read more about in on the interests page. Finally, you can listen to some of my music on the playlist page, just for fun. Enjoy!
