LOGAN KIMIKO MCKINLEY

Logan Kimiko McKinley is a mixed, Seattle-based visual artist.

I prefer traditional art forms such as drawing and painting, but I also practice digital art and web design.
My favorite art subjects are character design, stylized portrait drawing, and recently, realism.
I have been drawing consistently for about 4 years now, though I had brief interests in it previously.
I have always been interested in storytelling and writing, even before my interest in visual arts. Character design and art just gave me a new way to work on my world building and bring the people I created to life.
